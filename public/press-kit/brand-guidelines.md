# CGraph Brand Guidelines

## Positioning

CGraph is a privacy-first communication platform that combines secure messaging, community forums,
and engagement systems in one product.

## Voice

- Clear
- Technical but accessible
- Privacy-first
- Community-driven

## Color Direction

- Primary Emerald
- Secondary Purple
- Dark-first UI foundation

## Logo Usage

- Keep clear space around the mark
- Do not stretch or recolor the logo
- Use provided assets whenever possible
